# SEO Plan for Koshiv Roadlines

## SEO Strategy Overview
This SEO plan focuses on building a strong foundation for Koshiv Roadlines by targeting relevant road transport search intent groups, maintaining honest content, and preparing for future page-level expansion.

## Keyword / Search Intent Groups

### 1. Brand search
- Koshiv Roadlines
- Koshiv Roadlines transport
- koshivroadlines.in

### 2. Bus service intent
- bus service inquiry
- passenger transport service
- group travel bus service
- staff transport bus service
- school college bus transport inquiry

### 3. Truck service intent
- truck service inquiry
- goods transport service
- commercial load transport
- container movement inquiry
- business goods transport

### 4. Contract transport intent
- contract transport service
- company transport solution
- vendor transport support
- factory transport support
- regular goods movement

### 5. Route-based intent
- pickup to drop transport inquiry
- local transport inquiry
- intercity transport inquiry
- commercial route transport

### 6. Hinglish / common user searches
- bus service chahiye
- truck service chahiye
- maal transport service
- saman bhejne ke liye truck
- company staff bus transport
- goods transport inquiry

## Content and Metadata Guidelines
- Keep meta description natural and readable.
- Avoid keyword stuffing in title and description tags.
- Use keywords in page content and section headings when relevant.
- Focus on honest transport inquiry and service messaging.

## Page Priorities
- Homepage: core brand, bus service, truck service, contract transport, route inquiry.
- Future pages: Services, Bus Service, Truck Service, Contract Transport, Routes, About, Contact.
- Only add page URLs to SEO assets once pages exist.

## Link and Indexing Strategy
- Allow search engine indexing with a standard robots.txt file.
- Publish a sitemap that includes only the homepage for now.
- Keep canonical URL consistent on the homepage.

## Local / Hinglish Intent Notes
- Target common user search phrases in content sections and FAQ-style lines if needed.
- Avoid false regional claims and do not claim pan-India coverage.

## Future SEO Actions
- Verify the domain in Google Search Console after deployment.
- Submit sitemap.xml to search engines.
- Monitor URL inspection and indexing status.
- Add a Google Business Profile only when business details are finalized.
- Add address/phone details only after they are confirmed.
